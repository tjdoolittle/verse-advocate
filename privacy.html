Privacy Policy

Verse & Advocate — last updated July 10, 2026

Verse & Advocate collects no data. None.

What the app does

Verse & Advocate is a Bible verse search tool. All Bible text is bundled inside the app, and every search runs entirely on your device. The app has no server, no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party services.

What leaves your device

Nothing. Your searches, settings, and usage never leave your device. The app does not request network access for any feature beyond loading its own bundled content.

What is stored on your device

Two small preferences are saved locally so the app remembers them between visits: your chosen Bible translation and whether the topic suggestions are shown. These stay on your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing the app's data.

Children

Because no data is collected from anyone, no data is collected from children.
